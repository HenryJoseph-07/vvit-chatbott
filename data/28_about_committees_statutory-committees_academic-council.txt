Autonomous
Academic Council has been constituted with the Principal as the Chairman, three members from the University, experts from outside the college representing such areas as Industry, Education,Engineering etc., all heads of departments, senior faculty of the college and a member secretary. Academic Council is the principal academic body of this Autonomous Institute. It is responsible for laying down, regulating, and maintaining the standards of instruction, education and examination within the Institute. It has the right to advise the Governing Body on all academic matters.
