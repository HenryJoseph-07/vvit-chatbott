Examination Cell @ VVIT University
The Examination Cell at VVIT University is responsible for the smooth, transparent, and efficient conduct of all examinations. It ensures confidentiality, fairness, accuracy, and professionalism in all assessment-related activities. The Cell acts as a central link between students, departments, administration, and regulatory bodies, supporting a secure and student-friendly examination system.
Vision
To be a center of excellence in examination management, known for transparency, innovation, academic integrity, and reliability, thereby enhancing the reputation of VVIT University.
Mission
To conduct examinations professionally, fairly, and confidentially.
To use technology for scheduling, evaluation, monitoring, and timely result publication.
To promote academic integrity and accountability among all stakeholders.
To support departments in best practices in assessment and quality assurance.
To ensure a student-friendly, disciplined, and stress-free examination experience.
Organizational Structure & Duties
Designation
Duties & Responsibilities
Controller of Examinations (CoE)
Leads and oversees all examination operations.
Ensures compliance with university and regulatory guidelines.
Supervises scheduling, confidentiality, evaluation, and result declaration.
Approves revaluation, supplementary exams, and grievance redressal.
Represents Examination Cell in academic council and accreditation activities.
Deputy Controller of Examinations (DCoE)
Manages day-to-day examination operations and supports CoE in execution of processes
Coordinates seating, invigilation, and logistics.
Oversees secure handling of answer scripts.
Supports digital and OBE-based evaluations.
Assists in tabulation and result processing.
Assistant Controller(s) of Examinations (ACE)
Assists in scheduling, communication, and documentation.
Prepares hall tickets, seating plans, and invigilation charts.
Manages answer script collection and storage.
Maintains records and revaluation status.
Supports online exam processes and student assistance.
Department Examination Coordinators
Liaise between departments and Examination Cell.
Organize internal tests, mid exams, and practical exams.
Submit internal marks, attendance, and records on time.
Communicate schedules and exam instructions.
Report malpractice incidents and maintain exam records.
Technical Support & Data Management Team
Manages online exam platforms and digital portals.
Generates hall tickets and seating plans.
Ensures data security and result storage.
Provides technical support during exams.
Administrative & Support Staff
Manage logistics, documentation, and communication.
Arrange examination materials and support services.
Maintain student records and archives.
Core Functions
Planning and publishing examination schedules.
Conducting online, offline, theory, and practical exams.
Secure handling and storage of exam materials.
Result processing, grade sheet issuance, and transcripts.
Managing revaluation, supplementary exams, and grievances.
Ensuring discipline, confidentiality, and malpractice prevention.
Key Features
Technology-enabled examination management
Strong confidentiality and data protection.
Student-centric and time-bound services.
Compliance with accreditation standards.
Continuous improvement through innovation.
Commitment
The Examination Cell is committed to promoting fairness, academic discipline, accuracy, and credibility in the examination system, supporting the academic goals and values of VVIT University.
